About Me

I am a mechanical engineering student in my fourth year at Queen's University. Growing up in southern Ontario, I became interested in the neighbouring nuclear power plants. Combining that interest with my love of design, applied mathematics, and physics lead me to where I am today. Nearing the end of my undergraduate program, I plan to complete a MASc program focusing on the study of nuclear materials. I hope to continue my career within the nuclear industry upon completing my MASc program, aiding the international effort to decrease the need for fossil-fuel based energy.
